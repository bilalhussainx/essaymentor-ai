# Multi-Agent vs Single-Agent Results

## Performance Comparison

| Approach | Time | Quality Indicator |
|----------|------|-------------------|
| Single-Agent | 18s | Generic opening: "As I reflect on my academic journey..." |
| Multi-Agent | 87.5s | Specific opening: "**2:47 AM** As I stared at the endless lines of code..." |

## Agent Breakdown

- Research: 12.1s - Analyzes prompt deeply
- Brainstorm: 18.5s - Generates 5 unique ideas
- Outline: 20.2s - Structures narrative arc
- Draft: 18.7s - Writes with specific details
- Critique: 18.1s - Provides expert feedback

**Total: 87.5s for a college-ready essay**

## Key Insight

The multi-agent approach produces essays with:
- Specific details (exact times, concrete moments)
- Authentic voice (sounds like a real student)
- Clear narrative structure (researched and outlined first)
- Professional quality (Harvard-level critique included)

**Trade-off: 5x slower, but significantly better quality.**
